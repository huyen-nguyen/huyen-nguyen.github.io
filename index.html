Huyen N. Nguyen

Hi! I’m a Computer Science PhD student at Texas Tech University, advised by Prof. Tommy Dang.

My research interests include interactive visualization, visual analytics and human-computer interaction (HCI), supporting the understanding and insight discovery of data. In interactive Data Visualization Lab (iDVL), I develop web-based interactive visualization with D3.js and I study to construct a template for event detection mechanisms in time-series data. Details are in my CV.

In 2018, I received my B.S. in Information Systems from Hanoi University of Science and Technology, where I started with computer vision problems in object detection and object proposal generation.
